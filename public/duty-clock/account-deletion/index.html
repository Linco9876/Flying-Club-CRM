Request account deletion

BFC Duty Clock and Flight Management System

BFC Duty Clock uses the same account as the Bendigo Flying Club portal. Deleting it removes mobile and portal access and may affect other club services.

How to request deletion

  1. Email bfc@bendigoflyingclub.com.au from the email address registered to your account.
  2. Use the subject “Account deletion request” and include your full name.
  3. The club will verify your identity before making changes and will confirm the outcome by email.

What is deleted or retained

The club will deactivate authentication access and delete or anonymise personal profile data that is no longer required. Duty, flight, safety, financial or audit records may be retained where required for aviation safety, legal obligations, fraud prevention or legitimate record keeping. Where possible, retained records will be minimised or de-identified.

If you only want mobile access removed without deleting your portal account, say so in the request.
