BFC Duty Clock Privacy Policy

Effective 20 July 2026

Bendigo Flying Club operates BFC Duty Clock for authorised instructors and administrators. This policy explains the information used by the mobile app.

Information we collect

  • Account identifiers needed to sign in, including email address and internal user ID.
  • Duty records, including duty and break times, location labels, declarations, notes and flying hours.
  • Precise foreground device location, accuracy and nearest configured club location when the user opens and confirms Start duty.
  • Basic device platform information used in the duty audit record.

How information is used

Information is used to authenticate authorised staff, maintain duty and fatigue-management records, verify whether a duty start is at a configured club location, prefill logged flying time, support operational safety and investigate record discrepancies.

Location

The app requests location only while it is visible and a user is starting duty. It does not request background location, continuously track a user, use location for advertising or sell location information. If permission is declined, the user can enter a location and must explain the off-site or unavailable-GPS start.

Storage and sharing

Records are transmitted using encrypted connections and stored in the club's Supabase-backed flight management system. Supabase acts as a service provider supporting authentication and database hosting. Information is available only to authorised club personnel according to their system role. We do not sell personal information or use advertising SDKs.

Retention and deletion

Account and duty information is retained for as long as needed for club operations, aviation safety, dispute handling and applicable record-keeping obligations. A user can request account deletion or correction. Some duty, safety or audit records may need to be retained or de-identified where the club has a legal or legitimate operational requirement.

Your choices

You may decline location permission and provide the location manually with a note. To access, correct or request deletion of information, follow the account deletion instructions or contact the club.
