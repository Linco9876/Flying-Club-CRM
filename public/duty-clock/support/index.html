BFC Duty Clock Support

Help for Bendigo Flying Club instructors

Signing in

Use the same email address and password as the Bendigo Flying Club portal. Access is limited to instructors, senior instructors and administrators.

Location permission

Location is checked only when starting duty. If permission is unavailable or the device reports a location outside the club geofence, confirm the location name and add a short explanatory note.

Duty or flight-time corrections

Start time can be adjusted by up to two hours during clock-in. Flying hours are prefilled from the day's flight logs and remain editable when ending duty. Contact an administrator if a completed duty record needs correction.

Contact support

Email bfc@bendigoflyingclub.com.au or call (03) 5443 8395. Include your name, phone model, and a description of what happened. Do not email your password.
